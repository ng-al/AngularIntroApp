Unit Testing Custom Filters

Filters are the easiest of all recipes to test. This is because they are constructed via the $filter service and rarely do filters inject dependent services; so mocking is rarely required.

Building the filter

In a beforeEach() block:

  1. Inject the $filter service.
  2. Create the filter to test:
    var theFilter = $filter("filterRecipeName");
